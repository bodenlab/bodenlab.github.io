Rhys Newell

E-mail: rhys.newell@uqconnect.edu.au

Website: rhysnewell.github.io

LinkedIn: Rhys Newell

Rhys Newell is currently a PhD Candidate partially supervised by Benjamin Woodcroft, Gene Tyson, and Mikael Boden. Rhys completed his Honours degree in bioinformatics within the Boden Lab where he developed a tool to assess and refine reproducible peak regions using multiple ChIP-seq replicates called ChIP-R.

Currently, Rhys’ main work focuses on the development of novel methods to achieve strain-level metagenome-assembled genomes from metagenomic datasets in the form of a tool called Lorikeet.

Additionally, Rhys works on developing methods to quantify metabolite abundances from FTICR-MS data and correct sampling error between machine runs.
